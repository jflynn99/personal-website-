"All that smoke and savour rising so delicately from our altars. It leaves only ash behind".<br/><br/>Fantastic in both senses of the word. A really punchy and action packed modern retelling of some of the most famous Greek myths. <br/><br/>The titular Circe really comes alive, and despite her divine nature time and time again shows her humanity - from her touching motherly goodness to the rasping vengeance. <br/><br/>Follows the general outline of the classic Greek myths, so expect tragedy, some famous names dropping by, things not being all they seem, and unforeseen self torment.
